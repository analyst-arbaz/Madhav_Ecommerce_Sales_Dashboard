# 🛒 Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

> An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ecommerce sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and payment trends through insightful visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project built using **Power BI**. It transforms raw ecommerce transaction data into meaningful insights, enabling businesses to monitor sales, profit, customer purchases, category performance, and payment trends through dynamic visualizations.

This project demonstrates practical skills in **Data Cleaning, Data Modeling, DAX Calculations, and Dashboard Development**.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Sales Performance Analysis
- Monitor overall sales and profitability
- Analyze monthly profit trends
- Identify profitable and low-performing periods

### 🔹 Category Analysis
- Quantity sold by product categories
- Category-wise contribution to overall sales
- Comparative performance analysis

### 🔹 Sub-Category Profit Analysis
- Identify top profit-generating products
- Analyze profit contribution across sub-categories
- Support data-driven inventory decisions

### 🔹 Customer Insights
- Discover top customers by purchase amount
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify high-value customers

### 🔹 Payment Mode Analysis
- Analyze customer payment preferences
- Compare usage of COD, UPI, Debit Card, and Credit Card payment methods

### 🔹 Interactive Filtering
- Quarter-wise analysis using slicers
- Dynamic visualizations based on user selections

## 📌 Business Questions Answered

- What is the total sales revenue and profit?
- Which months generated the highest and lowest profits?
- Which product categories contribute the most to sales quantity?
- Which sub-categories generate the highest profit?
- Who are the top customers by purchase amount?
- Which payment methods are most preferred by customers?
- How does performance vary across different quarters?

---

## 💡 Key Insights

✅ Printers and Bookcases are the most profitable sub-categories.

✅ Clothing contributes the highest share of product quantity sold.

✅ COD is the most preferred payment method among customers.

✅ Profitability varies significantly across months, indicating seasonal sales patterns.

✅ A small group of customers contributes a major portion of total sales.
